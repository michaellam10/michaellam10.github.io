I believe that the things that hold greatest value in this life are not found in the product of our hands or in the reach of our wallets. But rather, the relationships, the conversations, the experiences that our hearts seem to innately cherish most deeply. Those which shape and mold us and remind us that there is greater joy to be had in striving for the immeasurable than things of finite value. I make it my aim to live this out and to encourage others towards this.

Hello there.

My name is Michael Lam. I am currently a junior at the University of Texas at Austin pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Arts & Entertainment Technologies. In my free time, I like to design and develop websites, play the piano, and enjoy nature. I am from Austin, Texas. I was born and raised here, and I now attend UT Austin, so Austin is definitely home for me. I may be a bit biased, but I fully believe that Austin is greatest city!

More important to me than my work and the things that I do are the relationships and experiences in my life that have had far greater and far deeper impact on my life. I am passionate about serving others; I am passionate about making a difference and leaving things better than I found them. I am passionate about not wasting my life.
