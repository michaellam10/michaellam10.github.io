Ignite Texas

Front-end Development | Design

Description

Ignite Texas, established in 2002, is a four-day retreat that connects incoming UT and ACC freshmen and transfer students to Christian community at The University of Texas and throughout Austin. This retreat provides students with friends and community before arriving to campus and also gives them the tools they need to seek spiritual growth during their time in Austin.

Contribution

Designed a new website for Ignite Texas and developed custom design from scratch using Squarespace's Developer Mode. Built front-end by writing custom Sass and JavaScript to target HTML generated by Squarespace’s graphical editor. Conducted periodic usability tests with media team to employ an iterative design process. Took ownership of seeing the new website through from ideation to launch.


Note: Previous version of website can be found here.

Technologies

HTML, Sass, JavaScript, jQuery, Responsive Design, Squarespace Developer Mode

Contribution
