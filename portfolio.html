Ignite Texas

Front-end Development | Design

Description

Ignite Texas, established in 2002, is a four-day retreat that connects incoming UT and ACC freshmen and transfer students to Christian community at The University of Texas and throughout Austin. This retreat provides students with friends and community before arriving to campus and also gives them the tools they need to seek spiritual growth during their time in Austin.

Contribution

Designed the look and feel of Ignite Texas's new website and developed a custom design from scratch using Squarespace's Developer Mode. Built in such a way that content can be updated through Squarespace without touching code, while preserving custom design. Took ownership of seeing the new website through from ideation to launch.

Technologies

CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Responsive Design, Squarespace Developer Mode

Travel Portal

Front-end Development | Design

Description

A responsive travel application that provides the user with photos, live weather information, a searchable map, and local activities and restaurants based on the searched destination. A one-stop resource for aggregating relevant information pertaining to travel destinations.

Contribution

Developed with ReactJS, using APIs from Yelp, Google, Unsplash, Dark Sky, and Giphy. Designed the look and feel of the application.


Note: Yelp's Fusion API only supports these regions, therefore locations outside of those supported will not include local activities and restaurants.

Technologies

HTML, CSS, Responsive Design, ReactJS
